# App Store Release Notes - Version 0.9.3

## What's New in Version 0.9.3

**Automatic Event Creation** - Events now auto-create when you publish your workouts to Nostr, making it easier to participate in fitness challenges.

**Expanded Charities** - Support 8 new Bitcoin circular economy communities from around the world! Now includes:
- Bitcoin Beach (El Salvador)
- Bitcoin Bay (Bay Area)
- Bitcoin Ekasi (South Africa)
- Bitcoin Isla (Philippines)
- Bitcoin Valley (Guatemala)
- Bitcoin District (Washington DC)
- Bitcoin Yucatan (Mexico)
- Bitcoin Veterans

Plus our original charities: OpenSats and Human Rights Foundation.

**iOS Permission Fix** - Fixed a freezing issue with the permissions modal on iOS for a smoother experience.

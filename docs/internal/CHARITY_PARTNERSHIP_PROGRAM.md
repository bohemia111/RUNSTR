## FAQ for Charity Partners

### Do we need to know about Bitcoin to participate?

**No!** We handle all the Bitcoin complexity. You just need a Lightning address (we'll help you set one up). Think of it like getting a PayPal accountâ€”easy to set up, instant to receive donations.

### What if our donors don't have Bitcoin?

**They don't need Bitcoin!** Most RUNSTR users pay with:
- Cash App (mainstream app, converts dollars to Bitcoin automatically)
- Strike (similar to Venmo, Bitcoin behind the scenes)
- Alby (Nostr-integrated wallet)

It's as easy as paying with PayPal, but with near-zero fees.

---

### Can we accept fiat donations too?

**Yes!** You can run traditional fundraising simultaneously. RUNSTR is an *additional* channel, not a replacement. Many charities do:
- RUNSTR events for Bitcoin/tech community
- GoFundMe for mainstream donors
- Direct bank transfers for major donors

---

### What if we don't hit our fundraising goal?

**No problem.** There are no penalties or fees. You keep 100% of whatever is raised, even if it's below your goal. We'll work with you to understand what worked and adjust for future races.

### How much promotion is expected from us?

**Charity Champion tier:** 2 email blasts + 5 social posts per race (2 races/year = ~14 touchpoints)

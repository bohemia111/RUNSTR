## What's New in Version 0.8.9

**Apple Run Tracker Fixed** - Resolved critical crash when starting Apple Run tracker. Your runs are now more stable and reliable than ever.

**Auto Stop for Presets** - Fixed automatic stopping functionality for preset workouts. Your training plans now work exactly as intended.

**Cleaner Activity UI** - Removed GPS display from activity tracking for a cleaner, more focused interface. Less clutter, more running.

**Fixed Split Announcements** - Voice announcements for run splits now play correctly, keeping you informed during your workout.

**Choose Your AI Coach** - Select from 10+ AI models for personalized coaching! Pick Claude Sonnet for detailed analysis, GPT-5 for quick responses, or DeepSeek for specialized insights. Find the perfect coach for your training style.

**Smarter Coaching** - Coach RUNSTR now has full access to RUNSTR project context, providing more relevant and accurate training advice. Your AI coach knows the app inside and out.

**Fitness Tests in History** - Your fitness test results now appear in your workout history and can be shared as Nostr posts. Track your progress over time.

**Enhanced Test Results** - Improved fitness test results screen with better data visualization and insights into your performance metrics.

**iOS Background Tracking** - New native HealthKit integration ensures unlimited background tracking during workouts. No more 30-minute limitations.

**Performance Boosts** - Faster workout card rendering, improved detail modal performance, and enhanced local storage efficiency.

Get fit, compete with friends, and earn Bitcoin rewards with RUNSTR!

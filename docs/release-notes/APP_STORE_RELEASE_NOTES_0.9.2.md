## What's New in Version 0.9.2

**Simplified Navigation** - Streamlined app experience with Stats moved to the front for quick access to your personal records and achievements.

**Clearer Labels** - Renamed "My Events" to "Events" and "My Workouts" to "History" for better navigation clarity.

**Anonymous Health Focus** - RUNSTR now focuses on being your private, anonymous health and fitness tracker powered by Nostr. Track your workouts, join RUNSTR-generated competitions, and earn Bitcoin rewards.

**Removed Teams** - Simplified the app by removing team-based features to focus on individual fitness tracking and RUNSTR-generated competitions.

**What is RUNSTR?**

RUNSTR is the first Bitcoin-powered fitness competition app. Track your workouts, compete in challenges, and earn Bitcoin rewards for staying active. All powered by the decentralized Nostr protocol - you own your data and your rewards.

**Key Features**:
- Track runs, walks, cycles, and other activities
- Join RUNSTR-generated fitness competitions
- Apple Health integration for automatic workout sync
- Share beautiful workout achievement cards
- Direct peer-to-peer Bitcoin payments
- Nostr-based social features
- Anonymous and private fitness tracking

Get fit, compete with friends, and earn Bitcoin rewards with RUNSTR!
